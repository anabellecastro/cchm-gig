About

The Design Team worked diligently to produce and design the overall aesthetic of the CCHM website. They envisioned the website to maintain a clean, modern appearance.

The team created a new color palette based on their existing logo, chose a new typeface, and designed icons. The icons were created to represent the "Gather, Save, Share" concept. They wanted to emphasize community in the design as well as something recognizable. They chose three of the primary colors from the color palette to feature them.
